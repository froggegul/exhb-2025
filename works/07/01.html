This work portrays the complex journey of a virtual character who gains a new life through advanced prosthetic technology. Born without sight and limbs, she acquires artificial eyes and limbs, but the price is becoming a human weapon deployed on the battlefield under government orders. Following the war, she transitions into a national symbol, performing the violin at military events and parades, eventually ascending to the prestigious stage of Carnegie Hall. The piece captures not only the new possibilities and hope afforded by cutting-edge technology but also the dystopian reality in which personal bodies and talents are controlled and instrumentalized for national purposes. It visually expresses a poignant melody of a beautiful yet sorrowful future, emerging at the intersection between individual accomplishment and governmental oppression.
