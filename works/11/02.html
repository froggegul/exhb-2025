This symbolic short film portrays a woman driven to self-destruction by her relentless pursuit of physical perfection in a society obsessed with appearances. With an anxious expression, she enters an elevator headed to an upscale cosmetic surgery clinic. After each operation, she briefly feels satisfaction with her enhanced beauty, only to spiral back into feelings of inadequacy upon encountering ever-more perfect faces in the media. As she continuously ascends and descends the elevator chasing ideal beauty, her face gradually distorts, becoming increasingly unrecognizable. Unlike the elevator moving only upward, her ultimate destination is downward, toward self-annihilation, where her sense of identity vanishes entirely. This film received the People's Choice Honoree award on September 4, 2025, at the Runway Gen:48 Aleph Edition, a short-film competition organized by New York-based generative AI video company Runway. It was recognized for both its artistic merit and profound social commentary. Notably, the entire production was completed within the competition's strict 48-hour time limit, powerfully demonstrating the capabilities and transformative potential of generative AI technologies.
