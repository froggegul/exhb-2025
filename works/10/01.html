Using generative AI technology, this piece reanimates old photographs of historical figures, creating faces that subtly move and express themselves once again. The artist, who has been deeply fascinated by generative AI since its early stages, creatively repurposed an AI tool originally offered as a supplementary feature on the genealogy platform MyHeritage.com, breathing new life into static images. In this work, faces from the past gently shift expressions and seem to gaze directly into our present. These moving portraits transcend simple visual recreations, blurring the boundaries between past and present, and opening a subtle space between memory and forgetting. Through this technologically crafted Alternate Reality, the artist prompts profound reflection on how we perceive, engage with, and remember history.
