This work reinterprets Charlie Chaplin's iconic 1940 film The Great Dictator through an early form of generative AI. The powerful message Chaplin conveyed over 80 years ago resonates strongly even today, revealing that humanity does not naturally grow wiser or more evolved over time; rather, we remain finite beings repeatedly prone to the same mistakes. Ultimately, the piece confronts viewers with fundamental questions: "What is real?" and "What truly matters?"
